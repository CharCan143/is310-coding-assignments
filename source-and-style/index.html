Fallout: New Vegas

Fallout: New Vegas is an open-world RPG set in a post-apocalyptic Mojave Desert. Players take on the role of a courier who is shot and left for dead, then must track down their attacker while getting caught up in a war between factions fighting for control of New Vegas.

Developed by Obsidian Entertainment, many of whose staff worked on the original Fallout games, New Vegas is praised for its emphasis on player choice and multiple story paths.
